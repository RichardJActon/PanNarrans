Rnotebooks - What and Why? For anyone unfamiliar with Rnotebooks here is a quick overview of why you might want to use them more experienced users can skip ahead. Rnotebooks are scientific notebooks for R, somewhat like jupyter for anyone coming from python but baked right into the Rstudio IDE which offers some benefits over the browser based interface of jupyter. It permits you to organise your code, notes, reasoning and references in one place.
